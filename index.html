What is NutriMom.AI

NutriMom is an AI-powered mobile app designed to provide personalized nutrition guidance for pregnant women and mothers with young children. By leveraging advanced AI and machine learning, NutriMom creates tailored meal plans, nutrient-rich recipes, and real-time tracking to ensure optimal health for both mother and baby. Users can log their meals, track essential nutrients, and receive instant feedback to stay on top of their health goals. The app also features a supportive community and expert consultations with certified nutritionists, empowering mothers with the knowledge and tools they need for a healthy pregnancy and early motherhood. With interactive learning modules, milestone-based insights, and adaptive recommendations, NutriMom is the ultimate companion for mothers looking to make informed dietary choices and nurture their child's growth.

Our Services

Personalization
  • Custom Nutrition Plans: AI-driven meal plans tailored to user profiles, including pregnancy stage, child's age, health conditions, and dietary preferences.
  • Dynamic Updates: Plans adapt based on real-time user feedback, such as food aversions or changes in health status.
  • Recommendation Engine: Offers personalized suggestions for meals and snacks, ensuring nutrient adequacy for maternal and child health
